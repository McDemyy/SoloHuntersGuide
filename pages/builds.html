Build Tips

⚡ GEAR SCALING

Always prioritize your main stat. Attack classes benefit most from critical rate + damage weapons. Defense classes need endurance + HP gear.

⚡ GATE RANK MATCHING

Match your build to the gate rank. E/D rank — any build works. B/A rank — require specialized builds. S rank — mythic gear strongly recommended.

⚡ SYNERGY

Full set bonuses unlock at 3 or 4 matching armor pieces. Mixing sets can break bonuses. Mixing works best only when higher rarity pieces compensate.

⚡ UPGRADES

Upgrade weapons first, then armor. A +10 Rare weapon often outperforms an unupgraded Epic weapon at lower levels.
